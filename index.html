Designer Production Services

Parametric Collection Design

Algorithmic and parametric jewelry design service. Generative form exploration across ring, pendant, and cuff typologies. Unique-per-piece collections with controlled variation — no two pieces identical.

Complex Geometry Production

Geometries impossible by hand or casting: interlocked kinetic chains, negative-space pendants, and topology-optimized ring structures. Direct SLM production in 18K gold, platinum, and silver.

Brand Production Runs

Consistent small-production runs for jewelry brands — 5 to 500 pieces per SKU. Identical geometry across the run with natural precious metal variation. Packaging and quality inspection before dispatch.
